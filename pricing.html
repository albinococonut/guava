Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have multiple shop locations?
Each license works for one shop location. If you operate multiple shops, you'll need a separate license for each location. Simply purchase additional licenses during checkout or contact us for volume pricing.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! Both monthly and annual plans can be cancelled at any time. Monthly plans stop at the end of your billing cycle. Annual plans are prorated if you cancel early—we're not in the business of keeping unhappy customers.
How long does setup take?
About 2 minutes. Install the Chrome extension, enter your license key and shop name, and you're ready to go. Your service advisors can start using Guava on their very next estimate.
What if my shop name changes in Tekmetric?
No problem! Just email us at support@getguava.ai and we'll update your license to match your new shop name within 2 hours during business hours.
Is there a free trial?
We don't offer free trials because we're confident Guava will transform how your advisors sell. However, we do offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not seeing results in the first two weeks, we'll refund you in full—no questions asked.
Do you offer training?
Guava is so intuitive, most advisors are using it successfully within their first call. But yes—we include email support and can provide a quick walkthrough video if your team needs it.
