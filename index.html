Yes. We built Guava specifically for Tekmetric because it's the industry standard. If you're on a different system, email us at support@getguava.ai and we'll let you know when we add support.
Do I need one license per advisor or per shop? +
One license covers your entire SHOP LOCATION. All advisors at that location can use it unlimited times per day. If you have multiple locations, you need one license per location.
How long does setup take? +
About 3 minutes. Install the Chrome extension, enter your license key, enter your shop name. Done. Your advisors can start using it immediately.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel in Stripe with one click. No penalties, no fees, no hoops. If you cancel, your license deactivates at the end of your billing period.
What if the script doesn't make sense? +
Hasn't happened yet, but if it does: your advisor can regenerate a new one with one click. The AI is trained on thousands of real automotive estimates and gets it right 99%+ of the time.
Will my advisors become dependent on it? +
Great question. What actually happens: Weeks 1-2 they read it word-for-word. Weeks 3-4 they start improvising and adding personality. Month 2+ they use it strategically for complex estimates and tough objections. You're not creating dependency—you're creating consistency.
What if I have multiple Tekmetric accounts? +
Each shop location gets its own license. So if you have "Bob's Auto - Downtown" and "Bob's Auto - West Side" as separate shops in Tekmetric, you need two licenses.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. We don't store customer data, VINs, or estimate details. Guava reads the page, generates the script, and that's it. Your data stays in Tekmetric.
